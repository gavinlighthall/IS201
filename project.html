2016 Premier League Season

Overview of the 2016 Premier League

The 2016 Premier League season was one of the most surprising and exciting in history. Leicester City, under the guidance of Claudio Ranieri, won the title in one of the most unlikely championship runs ever. Meanwhile, traditional powerhouses like Manchester United and Chelsea struggled, making way for fresh challengers. This season will forever be remembered for its drama, upsets, and unforgettable moments.

Final Standings (Top 5)

  • 1. Leicester City - 81 points
  • 2. Arsenal - 71 points
  • 3. Tottenham Hotspur - 70 points
  • 4. Manchester City - 66 points
  • 5. Manchester United - 66 points

Key Players to Watch in 2016

  1. 1. Top Scorers

    • Jamie Vardy (Leicester City) - The striker's incredible goal-scoring form was vital to Leicester’s success.
    • Harry Kane (Tottenham Hotspur) - A consistent goal-scorer and one of the league's best strikers.

  2. 2. Playmakers

    • Riyad Mahrez (Leicester City) - A standout performer with his exceptional dribbling and creativity.
    • Kevin De Bruyne (Manchester City) - His vision and passing were key to Manchester City's top-four finish.

  3. 3. Defensive Players

    • Toby Alderweireld (Tottenham Hotspur) - A rock in defense and a key part of Spurs’ strong defensive lineup.
    • Robert Huth (Leicester City) - Integral to Leicester's defensive success during their title-winning season.

Notable Matches of the 2016 Season

  • Leicester City 3–1 Manchester City - A pivotal match that showcased Leicester’s title credentials.
  • Tottenham Hotspur 2–2 Chelsea - The game that effectively ended Spurs’ title hopes and gave Leicester the crown.
  • Manchester United 3–2 Manchester City - A thrilling Manchester Derby with United edging out their rivals.
  • Arsenal 2–1 Leicester City - A dramatic comeback win that was one of the many high points of the season.
